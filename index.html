Ducati Panigale

Ducati Panigale V2 is a super bikes available at a starting price of Rs. 17,48,042 in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 2 colours. The Panigale V2 is powered by 955cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 152.8 bhp and a torque of 104 Nm. Engine Capacity: 955 cc Fuel Tank Capacity: 17 litres Transmission: 6 Speed Manual
